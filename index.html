ABOUT KNUCKLES

Knuckles is a heroic and very powerful echidna who lives on Angel's island, an island in the sky. He guards the island's giant Master Emerald

KNUCKLES CHARACTERISTICS

  1. Dislikes: Strong Sunshine
  2. Likes: Fruit, Digging holes
  3. Weak Point: Girls (He's a shy guy)
  4. Strong Point:' He can dig holes and climb cliffs with his spiked knuckles. He also has the ability to glide through the air.
  5. Age: 15
